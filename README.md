##  ✈️ SkyLink Travel Analytics Dashboard

##  📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end travel booking analytics solution built with **Power BI** to help business stakeholders monitor performance, identify revenue opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard analyzes booking trends, airline performance, travel demand, booking channels, and customer travel patterns to provide actionable business insights.

##  🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze travel booking data and answer key business questions such as:

- Which booking agencies generate the highest revenue?
- Which airlines contribute the most to overall revenue?
- Which destinations are the most profitable?
- How do monthly booking and revenue trends change over time?
- What is the distribution of one-way versus return trips?
- Which areas offer the greatest opportunity for business growth?

 ##  📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:
  - Executive KPI Cards
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Bookings
  - Average Ticket Value
  - Return Booking Rate
  - Lead Time
  - Revenue per Return Booking
  - Revenue Trend Analysis
  - Revenue by Booking Agency
  - Top 10 Airlines by Revenue
  - Trip Type Distribution
  - Top Destination Countries
  - Executive Summary
  - Business Recommendations
  - Interactive Slicers
  - Booking Agency
  - Trip Type
  - Custom Tooltip Page

##  🔍 Key Insights

- Expedia generated the highest booking revenue.
- Southwest Airlines recorded the highest airline revenue.
- The United States was the top-performing destination.
- Revenue peaked in October, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- Return trips accounted for nearly half of all bookings.
- Average ticket value remained stable throughout the reporting period.



##  💼 Business Recommendations

- Strengthen partnerships with high-performing booking agencies.
- Increase promotional campaigns before peak travel seasons.
- Expand marketing efforts in high-performing destinations.
- Improve customer retention through loyalty programs and personalized offers.
